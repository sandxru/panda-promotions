Meet the Founder

Our founder, Panida Promsook, created Panda Promotions with a goal for businesses to make genuine human connection with all of their clients. With years of experience in marketing, Panida found that connecting with people personally helped build a stronger bond through trust. Whether it is connecting business to business, to influencers, or simply to customers, human connections are able to be made everywhere!


We are passionate about helping our clients grow their brands and businesses! Using the approach of content marketing to grow brands, we have the ability to create customized packages for each and every client.
